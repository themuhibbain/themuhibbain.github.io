The Profile

The Muhibbain started to attract many listeners through a track titled Kunci Syurga "Key of Heaven", the first song written by Erwan Muhibbain (Erwan Taib) back in 2012. Kunci Syurga was in their first album, titled Mengenal Cinta which was distributed by Inteam Records. Kunci Syurga also managed to attract fans, especially listeners and lovers of spiritually inclined lyrics through song.

The History & The Name

Muhibbain founded and originally fronted by Erwan as a solo outfit, is now a duo, when Abdul Mutalib Nahar @ Adib (Fabulous Cats) accepted Erwan's invitation to join Muhibbain in mid 2013. Now Erwan and Adib call themselves, The Muhibbain.

The Muhibbain, or more accurately, Muhibbain is an Arabic name taken from the Arabic root word Hubb (حب), which means love and hence serves as the fundamental principal and overall drive as 'lovers' in spreading love.

Adib's Joining

According to Erwan, the timing of Adib's involvement as a Muhibbain couldn't be more perfect as both Erwan and Adib share the same mision and vision and compliment each other on and off the stage well. This, according to Erwan also, is crucial in their journey as The Muhibbain.

05 Nov 2014

Sejarah & Nama

Muhibbain yang mulanya ditubuhkan dan dianggotai oleh Erwan, telah berubah wajah apabila pertambahan seorang lagi anggota iaitu Abdul Mutalib Nahar @ Adib (The Fabulous Cats dan Almawlid) pada pertengahan tahun 2013. Kini, Muhibbain mahu dikenali sebagai The Muhibbain.

Nama The Muhibbain digarap dari perkataan Arab, Hubb yang membawa erti cinta, seterusnya memberikan maksud keseluruhan penubuhan kumpulan itu untuk membawa erti ‘pencinta’ kepada para pendengar dan pengikut mereka iaitu cinta sebenar kepada Allah SWT dan juga kekasihNya, Rasulullah SAW.

Adib Menyertai

Menurut Erwan, ketua Kumpulan The Muhibbain, penyertaan Adib sangat bertepatan dengan matlamat penubuhan kumpulan apabila mereka berkongsi misi dan visi yang sama dalam menyampaikan mesej positif kepada semua pihak tanpa mengira golongan dan peringkat.

"Our latest album titled #LoveCintaMahabbah is special to us first because we feel that we didn't leave anything behind… meaning, we brought all our past, the good, the not-so-good… all of it with us! But if you ask me, the sweetness of our latest album lies in between the tracks… literally and figuratively speaking," said Erwan.

"Not leaving anything behind is one thing… we've also tried to put in our present as much as we could. Many of our friends from the music side and also a dear friend from the academic side didn't hesitate to respond to our invitation to be featured in our latest album," said Adib.

And both Erwan and Adib agree that, although now they are not as young in age as before, but this chapter of their journey has just begun… and they hope to invite as many as possible to come and join them! InsyaAllah.
